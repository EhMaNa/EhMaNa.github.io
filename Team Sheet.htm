Team Sheet

A quick guide to the Liverpool First Team

Name: Alisson Becker
Position: Goalkeeper
Jersey Number: 1
Nationality: Brazil

Alisson Becker arrived to the club in July 2018 from AS Roma and enjoyed an incredible first season on Merseyside. Alisson was an immediate starter for the team, wearing the No.13 shirt. He made his official debut for Liverpool on 12 August, keeping a clean sheet in a 4–0 win over West Ham United. Alisson switched from No.13 to No.1 at the start of the 2019-20 campaign on April 2019.

Name: Virgil van Dijk
Position: Defender
Jersey Number: 4
Nationality: Netherlands

Virgil van Dijk arrived to the club in January 2018 from Southampton and helped the club to an European Final in that season. In his first full seson for the club, he helped clinch a sixth European Cup by being the heart of the defence. He made his debut for Liverpool on 5 January in the third round of the FA Cup and scored the winning goal with a late header in a 2–1 victory against local rivals Everton.

Name: Trent Alexander-Arnold
Position: Defender
Jersey Number: 66
Nationality: England

Trent Alexander-Arnold, a local lad and homegrown fotballer emerged from Liverpool’s Academy. The Scouser joined the club at the age of six and, after progressing through the ranks, the converted right-back was an integral figure in securing European Cup No.6. Alexander-Arnold made his professional debut on 25 October 2016, starting in a 2–1 victory over Tottenham Hotspur in the fourth round of the EFL Cup. He holds the record for most assists by a defender in a single season.

Name: Andrew Robertson
Position: Defender
Jersey Number: 26
Nationality: Scotland

Andy Robertson joined Liverpool following a transfer from Hull City on July 2017. He is now firmly established as one of the world’s premier full-backs and an indefatigable presence on the Reds’ left flank. On 19 August, Robertson made his debut in a 1–0 win over Crystal Palace, winning the Man of the Match award.

Name: Joe Gomez
Position: Defender
Jersey Number: 12
Nationality: England

Joe Gomez signed from Charlton Athletic in the summer of 2015, Gomez has featured regularly for the Reds at centre-back, right-back and left-back. At centre-back, he has formed a strong partnership with Virgil van Dijk. He made his debut against Stoke City on 9 August, starting as a left back in a 1–0 win, assisting Philippe Coutinho's match-winning goal in the 86th minute.

Name: Joel Matip
Position: Defender
Jersey Number: 32
Nationality: Cameroon

Joel Matip joined Liverpool in July 2016 from Schalke after his contract expired. He is more than comfortable with the ball at his feet and not afraid to venture forwards and that has made him a prominent figure for the side. Matip made his debut for Liverpool on 23 August 2016 against Burton Albion in the League Cup in a 5–0 win for the Reds.

Name: James Milner
Position: Midfielder
Jersey Number: 7
Nationality: England

James Milner agreed to join Liverpool on a free transfer from Manchester City on 4 June 2015. Milner’s leadership, experience, professionalism and versatility makes him an integral member of the squad. Though his preferred position is midfield, he has been used as a full back and winger. Milner played his first competitive match for the club in a 1–0 win over Stoke City.

Name: Fabinho
Position: Midfielder
Jersey Number: 3
Nationality: Brazil

Full name Fabio Henrique Tavares, is a patrolling defensive midfielder who agreed to join Liverpool from AS Monaco on May 28, 2018 and officially becoame a Liverpool player on July 1. His dominant performances at the base of midfield led to him becoming an integral member of the team. Fabinho made his debut for the club as an injury-time substitute for Sadio Mané in a Champions League group match against Paris Saint-Germain on 18 September, a 3–2 win at Anfield.

Name: Jordan Henderson
Position: Midfielder
Jersey Number: 14
Nationality: England

Jordan Henderson joined Liverpool on 9 June 2011 from Sunderland. He became the club’s vice-captain in September 2014, before he then took the armband on a permanent basis in July 2015 following Steven Gerrard’s departure. He is a player whose physical, mental and footballing gifts enable him to excel in a variety of midfield roles. Henderson made his debut in a 1-1 draw against his former club Sunderland in the Premier League.

Name: Georginio Wijnaldum
Position: Midfielder
Jersey Number: 5
Nationality: Netherlands

Georginio Wijnaldum arrived to Liverpool from Newcastle United on July 2016. He is a talented, versatile all-round midfielder that has inserted into the side from the very start he joined. Wijnaldum made his debut for Liverpool against Arsenal on 14 August 2016, where he played 80 minutes and provided an assist for Adam Lallana before being replaced.

Name: Sadio Mane
Position: Forward
Jersey Number: 10
Nationality: Senegal

Sadio Mane joined Liverpool from Southampton on 28 June 2016. He is a flexible forward who allies lightning pace and ruthless finishing with acute tactical awareness, Mane primarily operates from the left of the devastating front three. On 14 August, he made his debut for the Reds, scoring the fourth goal in a 4–3 away win against Arsenal.

Name: Mohamed Salah
Position: Forward
Jersey Number: 11
Nationality: Egypt

Mohamed Salah joined Liverpool from AS Roma on June 22 2017. Salah is a wide player who can play in a more central position and is capable of scoring a variety of goals, from spectacular long-range efforts to poacher’s finishes, as well as creating them too. On 12 August, he made his debut for the Reds, scoring against Watford in a 3–3 draw.

Name: Divock Origi
Position: Forward
Jersey Number: 27
Nationality: Belgium

Divock Origi signed from Lille on 29 July 2014 to Liverpool. Divock Origi can play on all sides of the front three and he nearly always makes an impact when called upon. His first competitive game for the team was on 12 September, replacing Danny Ings in a 3-1 loss at Manchester United.

Name: Roberto Firmino
Position: Forward
Jersey Number: 9
Nationality: Brazil

Roberto Firmino signed from Hoffenheim on 23 June 2015 to Liverpool. He is a player with physical gifts that complement his exceptional footballing talents, he leads the line of the devastating front three with a fearsome and influential combination of industry and ingenuity. Firmino made his debut in a 1–0 win at Stoke City replacing Jordon Ibe.

Name: Naby Keita
Position: Midfielder
Jersey Number: 8
Nationality: Guinea

Liverpool secured an agreement with RB Leipzig for the transfer of Naby Keita on August 29, 2017. Keita is a dynamic, box-to-box central midfielder, able to distribute the ball with range and accuracy, and can score goals too. Keïta made his debut for Liverpool in a 4-0 win against West Ham at Anfield.

Name: Alex Oxlade-Chamberlain
Position: Midfielder
Jersey Number: 15
Nationality: Englaand

Alex Oxlade-Chamberlain joined Liverpool from Arsenal on 31 August 2017, the final day of the 2017 summer transfer window. His attacking intent, crossing and a knack to hit long ranges shot has made him one of the integral members of the team. On 9 September 2017, Oxlade-Chamberlain made his debut for Liverpool, coming on as a substitute in a 5–0 loss to Manchester City.

Name: Jurgen Klopp
Position: Manager
Nationality: Germany

On 8 October 2015, Jurgen Klopp agreed a three-year deal to become Liverpool manager. The charismatic German led the team to European and World glory by securing Liverpool's 6th European trophy, a Super Cup trophy and for the first time a Club World Cup trophy. He set his sight to the Premier League and now has ended the Reds’ 30-year wait for a league title in the summer of 2020. Klopp's debut was a 0–0 away draw with Tottenham Hotspur on 17 October 2015.
